Qurban in China Organised by Mariah Mah

2025
Eid Adha Qurban

Zhang Jiachuan Charity, Gansu Province

140 healthy sheep weighing between 40-50 kg were purchased for ibadah Qurban on 7 June 2025.

The sheep were then transported to a designated place where Qurban would be carried out accordingly.

Healthy cows weighing not less than 500 kg were purchased for Qurban.

Slaughter was invoked in the name of ALLAH the Great and Merciful! ALLAHUAKBAR!!!

After prayers, each sheep was duly slaughtered in the name of the donor.

Each cow was also slaughtered in the name of the donor.

The cows were skilfully skinned off their hide.

The slaughtered cows were hacked to several portions and moved to the kitchen to be cut into smaller pieces.

Next, all the slaughtered sheep were lined up to be skinned.

After the sheep have been skinned clean, they were brought to the kitchen...

...where a slicing machine helped to cut the meat into small pieces for easy packing.

Volunteer workers assisted in weighing and packing 2.5 kg of meat in each plastic bag for distribution.

Meat packs were loaded onto vehicles for distribution to far off rural areas.

Volunteers deliver Qurban meat to the poor, the sick and invalids who live in the outskirts.

They had to travel miles of undulating rough narrow roads and paths to reach their homes.

Qurban meat were delivered to homes of invalids, many of whom are bed ridden.

Poor villagers are grateful for meat which they rarely could afford to buy.

Qurban meat was also cooked in the kitchen for immediate consumption, to be served to the elderly residents of Old Folks Home and children in the Orphanage.

Qurban meat was also cooked in the kitchen for immediate consumption, to be served to the elderly residents of Old Folks Home and children in the Orphanage.

Alhamdulillah! Meat was cooked tender and soft after 3 hours of boiling.

On Eid Adha Day, our old folks at the Home were served piping hot Qurban meals by our volunteer workers.

Elderly residents at the Home were blessed with a meal of Qurban meat! Alhamdulillah!

Alhamdulillah! Qurban meat dishes were also served to children at the Orphanage.

Volunteers helped the orphaned children with the food.

The orphans relished a hearty Qurban meal.

Alhamdulillah and thank you donors for your Qurban! May ALLAH bless your ibadah with abundant barakah! Ameen!
